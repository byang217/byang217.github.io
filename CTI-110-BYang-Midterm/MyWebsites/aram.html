ARAM


All Random All Mid, commonly referred to as ARAM, is one of the mini games that League has to offer.


The objective of this game is similar to Summoner's Rift however, there's only 1 lane for all players to utilize, the mid lane.


This is my favorite game mode because it focuses less on farming tactics and more on fighting the enemy team.


In addition, ARAM games typically consume less time because of it's faster-paced nature.

Teemo Katarina Lucian


My 3 favorite champs to play in ARAM are Teemo, Kat, and Lucian


Teemo, because he looks like an innocent champion but, has the potential to exert a lot of damage. His mushroom traps are super annoying to walk into.


Katarina, because her play style requires a good amount of skill to be successful. However, she is a very strong champion when played correctly.


Last but not least, Lucian. He is an ADC champion that also requires a good amount of skill. He has a dash ability that is really fun to use to close the gap between enemies.
