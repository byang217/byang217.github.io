I created this Website to give you, the audience, an brief insight into my favorite game, League of Legends.

League of Legends, commonly referred to as "LOL" or League, is a free Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA) game.

According to Wikipedia.org, the game was first released on October 27, 2009 by Riot Games. Throughout the years, League has expanded globally and even has a professional eSports presence. Although this game is incredibly fun, it is also infamous for it's toxic player community. In my opinion, all online groups are subject to toxicity. If you enjoy the game, just mute all :P.
