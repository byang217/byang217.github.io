Summoner's Rift


Summoner's Rift is a 5 vs 5, strategic co-op MOBA game.


Each player chooses one of the hundreds of available champions. A champion is a character in the game. All champions have unique attibutes, abilities and play-styles.

Essentially, a player will choose a champion based on their role. Your role will designate what lane you will primarily reside in. The 5 roles on each team are as follows:


  • Top Laner
  • Jungler
  • Mid Laner
  • ADC
  • Support
Nexus Inhibitor Turret


Ultimately, the objective of the game is to work with your team to conquer the enemy and destroy their Nexus.


Before you can destroy the enemy Nexus, you must first destroy 1 of 3 enemy inhibitors.


And ofcourse before that, you must destroy the enemy turrets, guarding such structures.
